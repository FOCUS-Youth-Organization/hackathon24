Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event that usually last several days, where programmers work together in teams in order to create a project that often with a focus on innovation and problem solving

What should I bring?

Please bring devices, chargers, a water bottle, and anything else you think you may need

Who can come to FOCUS Hacks?

At Focus HACKS, our Hackathons are open to programmers of all levels of coding experience, ranging from beginners to experts.

What are the prizes?

We offer prizes of up to $500 of value, teams and individuals will be awarded separately

What is the maximum team capacity?

The maximum team capacity for our Hackathons are three people. It is fine to work alone, but if you are unable to find a team and prefer to have one, we are able to help you find a team

How can I stay up to date with FOCUS Hacks?

If you would like to stay up to date with us, please follow the official FOCUS Youth Instagram account
